Installation

Download and install MGenie on your Mac in seconds.

Step 1

Download MGenie

Click the button below to download the latest release for your Mac:

Download MGenie.dmg
ℹ️ Works on macOS 13+ · Apple Silicon & Intel · Free download

Step 2

Open the Installer

Your browser should download MGenie.dmg. Open it from your Downloads folder. You'll see the MGenie icon and an Applications folder.

Step 3

Drag to Applications

Drag the MGenie app icon into the Applications folder. You can also just double-click the app icon to launch it directly from the .dmg.

Step 4

Verify & Launch

Navigate to your Applications folder and double-click MGenie to launch it. On first launch, macOS may ask for permission — click Open.

✅ You may see a notification about an unidentified developer. Click Open to trust the app. You only need to do this once.

Permissions

MGenie will ask for the following permissions on first use:

  • Microphone: For recording and transcription
  • Speech Recognition: For live-to-text
  • Accessibility (optional): Only for global keyboard shortcuts

You can grant or deny these in System Settings → Privacy & Security at any time.

Updates

MGenie checks for updates automatically. When a new version is available, you'll see a notification in the menu bar. Download and run the new .dmg to update.

Or visit GitHub Releases to download any version manually.

Uninstall

To uninstall MGenie, simply drag the app from Applications to Trash. All scripts and recordings are saved in ~/Documents/MGenie/ and are not removed.
