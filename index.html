Everything you need on stage

🪟

Transparent overlay

Floats above all windows. Drag and resize it anywhere on any screen — position persists across launches.

🎙️

Live transcription

Records and transcribes your speech in real time using Apple's on-device speech recognition. No cloud required.

🤖

AI answers

Sends your transcript to an LLM and streams the answer back — visible on screen while you keep talking.

🔊

Text-to-speech

Reads your script aloud with word-level highlighting and scroll sync. Rehearse or present hands-free.

⌨️

Global hotkeys

⌃⌥H show/hide · ⌃⌥P play/pause · ⌃⌥C control panel. Works without Accessibility permissions.

📚

Knowledge base

Every session — script, transcript, and AI answers — exported as Markdown to a folder you own.
